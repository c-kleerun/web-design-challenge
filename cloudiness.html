Latitude vs Cloudiness

This graph shows the comparison of latitude and cloudiness. Based on the data represented in the graph, we cannot predict the cloudiness of a location based on latitude. There do seem to be some smilarities in how certain locations identify the percentage of cloud cover. These are represented as clusters of data points centered along horizontal lines. This seems to indicate that several cities across latitudes report 0% cloudiness, or 75% cloudiness.
