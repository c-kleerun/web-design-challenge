Latitude vs Wind Speed

This graph shows the comparison of latitude and wind speed. Based on the data represnted in the graph, we cannot infer how windy a location will be based on latitude alone. The data points are scattered fairly evenly across the range of wind speeds and latitudes. You can tell that most cities seem to experience wind speeds under 25 miles per hour. We can also infer that wind speeds above 25 miles per hour are outliers.
